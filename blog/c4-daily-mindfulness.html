Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I popped in unexpectly to see a friend I hadn't seen in a while and tried some of the techniques suggested. Firstly she was surprised to see me and I realised as I focused on listening that I'm not used to giving my undivided attention. My brain is always thinking of what else needs to done. As she talked and I listened I tried to remember to listen, loop and dip. I personally learnt that sometimes I assume things out of habit and it's a habit I'd like to try and break. Overall we both felt good about our conversation however I think she genuinely felt grateful that I had randomly popped over as I hadn't seen her in a while. Will most definitely try and incorporate these technique into my everyday life.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

The walking practice is a technique I will try. Any technique that requires physical activity is beneficial.
